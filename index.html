Breaking Beats at Sonic Bloom Fest

Sonic Bloom Fest comes alive from first light to flame nighttent, weaving together live performances, immersive visuals, and shared moments of pure musical connection.

Wander between vibrant stages and intimate lounges—each space pulsing with energy, surprise collaborations, and the joy of being part of a collective experience.

This is more than a festival; it’s a community united by the power of music, where every beat, melody, and chorus becomes a story you’ll carry long after the last encore.

Live Moments

Dive into a curated selection of standout performances— from sunrise Live Moments that gently awaken the senses to late-night sets that transform the grounds into a sea of movement and light. Whether you’re drawn to genre-bending pioneers, rising stars making their mark, or iconic acts that defined a generation, every performance is handpicked to stir emotion and spark connection. Discover hidden gems on tucked-away stages, revel in crowd-favorite anthems, and lose yourself in the pulse of music that defines the heart of Sonic Bloom Fest.

Main Stage Spectacle

Witness headline acts under dazzling lights and thunderous sound, delivering performances crafted to move every corner of your heart and feet. From the first pulse of bass to the final hands-in-the-air chorus, each set is a full-sensory experience—light, sound, and crowd energy fused into something unforgettable.

Lounge & Chill

Retreat to shaded corners and cozy lounges, where laid-back grooves, intimate showcases, and surprise acoustic sets offer moments of calm and connection. Sink into beanbags under string lights, sip something cold, and let the slower rhythms guide you into deep conversations, quiet smiles, and unexpected musical discoveries.

Afterglow Anthems

Lose yourself in the pulse of late-night DJ sets that blur genre lines and stretch into the early hours. Beneath a canopy of stars, deep grooves, euphoric drops, and communal movement turn the dancefloor into a shared rhythm—where every track feels like a final encore you never want to end.

Weekend Lineup Preview

From sunlit afternoons to electric nights, our weekend preview guides you through the festival’s rhythm—plan your journey, mark your must-see moments, and prepare for an unforgettable adventure. Expect a dynamic mix of crowd favorites, underground icons, and surprise pop-ups across every stage. Whether you’re here for high-energy headliners or laid-back daytime sets, the weekend is packed with discoveries waiting to be made.

Festival Eats

Embark on a culinary journey through the heart of the festival, where rows of artisan food trucks, sizzling pop-up kitchens, and adventurous roaming vendors await. Discover global flavors and local favorites—from spice-laden street tacos bursting with heat, to slow-cooked barbecue, fragrant noodle bowls, and colorful, plant-based creations sourced from nearby farms. Whether you’re craving comfort food, curious fusions, or something refreshingly unexpected, every bite becomes part of the experience.
