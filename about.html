Where It All Began: Sonic Bloom Fest

Sonic Bloom Fest began over a decade ago as a grassroots gathering of electronic music lovers, growing from a small lakeside stage into one of Europe’s most anticipated cultural celebrations.

What started as a weekend experiment now spans multiple days and genres, hosting legendary acts, rising stars, and unexpected reunions that have shaped its evolving identity.

More than just an event, Sonic Bloom Fest has become a living archive of modern music—its history etched into every beat, every cheer, and every shared memory under open skies.

History

In the summer of 2012, a group of electronic music enthusiasts transformed a quiet lakeside meadow into an impromptu dance floor. What started as a floating gathering of friends with music and lights quickly drew a curious crowd. Inspired by the response, the founders returned the next year with a proper lineup and built stages from abandoned docks and forest clearings. That was the beginning of Sonic Bloom Fest, now a multi-day celebration of cutting-edge music, immersive art and community beneath the open sky.

Festival FAQs

What should I pack for the festival?

Bring a reusable water bottle, ear protection, weather-appropriate clothing, and a portable phone charger. A blanket or foldable chair can come in handy for chill sets, and don’t forget your ticket and ID!

How can I discover new artists?

Use the official app or printed schedule to see set times and artist bios. Wander into smaller stages for hidden gems and follow the crowd’s energy—you might just find your next favorite act.

Where are the best hangout spots?

Chill zones near food courts, shaded lounges by the art installations, and hilltop viewpoints are great for relaxing between sets. Ask volunteers or returning attendees for tips.

What time of day has the best vibe?

Mid-afternoon is great for catching energy-building sets, while sunset brings magical transitions. Late-night is when the dance floors ignite and surprise acts appear.
